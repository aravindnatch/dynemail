Welcome to Dynamic Email!

This application allows you to change the content of your emails!

How it Works:
Traditionally changing the text of your email is not allowed as script loading is considered malicious. This application leverages HTML email's dynamic image loading by converting written text into an image. This image can then be later changed and even deleted from this website's CDN.

How to Use:
1. Enter your Gmail credentials (enable less secure apps)
2. Fill out the rest of the form
3. Press the 'Send Email' button
